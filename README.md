# Explainable Decision Making for Property-for-Debt Investment: A Symbolic Regression Approach
The Chinese construction industry has faced unprecedented challenges since 2020, with ``property-for-debt" transactions exceeding 420 billion yuan in 2023 alone, creating critical decision-making dilemmas for enterprises like H Company. Traditional black-box machine learning approaches, while offering predictive capabilities, fail to provide the transparency required for high-stakes financial decisions. This study addresses this fundamental challenge by developing a scientific-grade PhySO symbolic regression framework that generates explicit mathematical formulas for investment decision-making. Our dual-task learning approach simultaneously handles investment classification (whether to accept property compensation) and profit prediction (expected financial outcomes), discovering two groundbreaking mathematical relationships: a classification formula achieving perfect decision accuracy (100\%) and a regression formula with exceptional predictive power enhanced through linear calibration ($R^2$ improvement from 0.22 to 0.784, representing 258\% relative enhancement). The discovered classification formula, $\frac{X_4^2 \times \cos^2(X_0)}{X_2^2}$, directly relates commercial rental income, property management costs, and financing expenses, providing business stakeholders with interpretable decision rules. Evaluations using 71 real investment projects demonstrate that our scientific-grade PhySO config2 framework achieves exceptional performance while maintaining complete mathematical transparency. This research bridges theoretical advances with practical applications, offering construction enterprises a transparent, mathematically rigorous tool to navigate complex investment decisions while meeting regulatory compliance requirements.
